Commonly asked questions

What is the last date to file my income tax return?

The due date usually depends on whether you need a tax audit. For most individuals, it's 31st July of the assessment year. Filing on time helps avoid penalties and interest.

What documents do I need to file my income tax return?

Common documents include your Form 16 (from employer), bank statements, TDS certificates, investment proofs, and details of other income. Having these ready makes filing quick and accurate.

Can I claim deductions to reduce my taxable income?

Yes, under sections like 80C, 80D, and 80G, you can claim deductions for investments, insurance premiums, and donations. Properly claiming deductions lowers your tax liability legally.
